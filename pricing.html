Will I be able to cancel unused accounts?

Yes - you are free to cancel at any time. To cancel, go to your billing page and click "Please cancel my account" at the bottom of the page. To cancel an account, you will need to be an administrator/owner. An email confirmation will be sent upon cancellation request.

New accounts are the only ones that are eligible for the 30-day trial. If you upgrade an existing account, you will be billed the new amount when your next billing cycle starts.
